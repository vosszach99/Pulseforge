Pulseforge

  • Energy Manipulation
  • Advanced Combat Suit
  • Machine Interface
  • Protective Father

Pulseforge, also known as Ethan Grayson, is an engineer-turned-superhero who uses his energy-based abilities and advanced combat suit to fight the Eclipse Syndicate and protect his family. A beacon of hope, he balances heroics and fatherhood with determination and heart.
