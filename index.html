About Pulseforge

Pulseforge is the alter ego of Ethan Grayson, a father, engineer, and defender of hope. With powers to manipulate energy and the advanced Energy Vanguard Suit, he battles the sinister Eclipse Syndicate to protect the world and his loved ones.

Backstory

My name was Ethan Grayson, a dedicated engineer and father who spent years developing cutting-edge energy solutions to power the world. One fateful night, an experiment involving a prototype energy reactor went horribly wrong. A catastrophic energy explosion didn’t kill me but instead merged the reactor’s advanced technology with my body and mind, forever altering me.

The transformation gave me the ability to manipulate energy at will and interface with machines seamlessly. When I discovered the explosion was sabotage by the shadowy Eclipse Syndicate, I created the Energy Vanguard Suit to amplify my powers and shield myself from the immense energy surging through me.

Now, as Pulseforge, I fight against the Syndicate’s tech-enhanced mercenaries while protecting my family and inspiring a brighter future. The world sees me as a glowing beacon in the night, but to my son, I’m still just Dad—his greatest hero.
